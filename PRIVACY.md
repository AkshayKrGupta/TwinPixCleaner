# Privacy Policy for TwinPixCleaner

**Last Updated: November 30, 2025**

## Overview

TwinPixCleaner ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your data.

## Data Collection

**TwinPixCleaner does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

### What We Access

The App requires access to:
- **File System**: To scan folders for duplicate images
- **Photo Library**: To scan your Photos library (optional)
- **Trash/Bin**: To safely move deleted files

### How We Use This Access

- **Local Processing Only**: All scanning and duplicate detection happens entirely on your Mac
- **No Cloud Upload**: Your files never leave your device
- **No Analytics**: We don't track your usage or collect statistics
- **No Third Parties**: No data is shared with any third-party services

## Permissions Required

### Full Disk Access (Optional)
- Required to scan system folders and external drives
- You can grant this in System Settings → Privacy & Security → Full Disk Access
- The App will function with limited access if not granted

### File Access
- The App only accesses folders you explicitly select
- No background scanning or automatic access to your files

## Data Storage

- **No Remote Storage**: All data stays on your Mac
- **No User Accounts**: No login or registration required
- **No Cookies**: The App doesn't use cookies or tracking technologies

## Your Rights

You have complete control over:
- Which folders the App can scan
- Which files to delete
- When to use the App

## File Deletion

- Deleted files are moved to macOS Trash/Bin
- You can recover deleted files from Trash before emptying it
- The App does not permanently delete files

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Changes will be posted in the App and on our website.
